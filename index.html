About Us

We are a student-run project at Freedom High School in Tampa, FL, creating school-approved T-shirt designs with true Patriots pride. All orders are fulfilled right here on campus.

Our T-Shirts

How We Make Our T-Shirts

At Freedom High School's student business, we handle the full process with pride:

  1. Design – Brainstorm and create Patriots-themed artwork using school colors.
  2. Proof & Approval – Make digital mockups and get teacher/student approval.
  3. Printing – Use screen printing, heat press, or vinyl cutting on campus.
  4. Quality Check – Every shirt is inspected for perfect quality.
  5. Packaging & Sales – Packaged and ready for you to pick up or wear with pride!

Want to help or suggest a design? Talk to us in class or via contact!
