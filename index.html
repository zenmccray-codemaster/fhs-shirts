How We Make Our T-Shirts

We use two professional printing methods depending on the design and shirt type. Here's how they compare:

Feature Sublimation Printing DTF (Direct to Film)
Best For Light-colored polyester shirts Any color shirt (cotton, poly, blends)
Color Vibrancy Extremely vibrant, no feel Very vibrant, slight texture
Durability Excellent (dyes into fabric) Very good (with proper curing)
Feel on Shirt No texture (feels like part of shirt) Slight raised feel
Wash Fastness Will not crack or fade Very resistant to cracking
Design Complexity Unlimited colors, gradients Unlimited colors, fine details

We choose the best method for your design to ensure the highest quality Patriots gear!
